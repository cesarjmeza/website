At San Francisco State University (SFSU), I served as _Instructor of Record_ for Precalculus, Business Calculus, and Mathematics for Statistical Quantitative Reasoning.
Currently, I am an _Associate Member_ in the [Professional Development in Teaching Program](https://ctl.wustl.edu/programs/professional-development-in-teaching-program/) hosted by the WashU [Center for Teaching and Learning](https://ctl.wustl.edu/).
In 2024, I earned the WashU Department of Mathematics _Brian Blank Teaching Award_.

In the summers of 2022-2024, I served as _Mathematics Instructor_ for the [Pomona College Academy for Youth Success (PAYS)](https://www.pomona.edu/administration/draper-center/pays).
